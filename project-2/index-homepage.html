Muskie Fishing

Muskie and Pike fishing is enjoyed by a smaller more niche group of individuals. With Muskie and Pike being apex predators fewer of them can occupy a body of water which makes them drastically more elusive. Although it’s more difficult to target Muskie and Pike, the pay off of eventually hooking up with one makes it so much sweeter. Getting into this type of fishing requires stronger tackle than other types of fishing.

Catfish Fishing

Catfishing is widely excepted as the more leisurely form of fishing. When targeting catfish there tends to be more down time. Since these fish use they’re whiskers to feed, it makes it harder to find the bait. Getting into catfishing is relatively simple and does not require extensive tackle.
